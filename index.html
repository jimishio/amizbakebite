Meet Our Chef

Ami Pandya

Follow your passion - says everyone around but very few actually do!

Ami Pandya is one of those - An MBA with passion in baking, leaves the corporate job, takes the leap of faith and sets up her own bakery!

A little girl standing in her mom's kitchen, sniffing one flavour at a time grew up to make her own wonderland filled with desserts. Ami Pandya is a baker and an explorer of sorts who has a knack of experimenting with food and creates wonders around it. As a child she was amazed with the variety of flavours that can be created with the right amount of mix! Her first memory of baking roots down to the school days where she made birthday cakes for her friends and teachers.

As a career, she pursued graduation and after getting her MBA degree she took up a 9 to 5 job. Sooner than later she realised her calling. One fine day, she made up her mind, and quit high profile corporate job! "Lets Bake" is what she told to herself and then the journey of Baking began!

Ami took up professional training in patisserie and returned to her hometown to establish her own setup. After putting much thought into it, She finally commenced her own business. Being one of a kind in Bhavnagar, she had an opportunity to present a plate full of fresh and quality desserts. Moreover, She not only conducts professional workshops on baking but also is featured twice in the popular TV show "Rasoi show" flaunting her very own fusion recipes! Now, it's a landmark of her own!

That is how "Ami’s BakeBite" came into existence. It has been 3 years of catering sweetness, love and sheer diligence but still it feels like it was yesterday. The zeal, passion for bringing out the right flavour and taste is visible in her work. Now, everytime she enters the kitchen its MAGIC! The oven starts to warm up to bring out the best cakes and cookies in town!
