About The Baker

Ami Pandya

A little girl standing in her mom’s kitchen, sniffing one flavour at a time grew up to make her own wonderland filled with desserts. Ami Pandya is a baker and an explorer of sorts who has a knack of experimenting with food and create wonders around it. Her first memory of baking roots down to making birthday cakes for her friends when she was sweet sixteen.

After getting her MBA degree she took up a 9 to 5 job but was yet to realize where her true calling was. With all the guts she quits her full-time job and moves towards glory as she apprehends her passion for baking. She decided to take up professional training in patisserie. This opened new doors for Ami. She decided to start her own dessert boutique as a home baker in her hometown Bhavnagar. Being one of a kind in the city, she had an advantage of tapping the virgin market and thereby presenting a plate full of fresh and quality desserts.

After putting much thought into it, Ami finally went leaped and commenced her passion into a business and that is how Ami’s Bake Bite came into existence. It started on 25th of April, 2016 and it has been 3 years of catering sweetness, love and sheer diligence.
